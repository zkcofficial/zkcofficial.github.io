# Weekly Recap

<hr>

This week has been fun - a little much, but fun<br>
This week I have started to plan when and where I can go volunteer, and hopefully the choices I picked out are solid and wll work<br>
This week I decided I am going to start davening more; before I was just putting on Tefillin and saying Shema, maybe saying the 
appropriate prayers for either Shacharit Mincha or Ma'ariv, when able. Now I am going to challenge myself to start 
saying EACH of the three daily prayers every day for this week. Otherwise, Shabbat was fun, mostly slept but when I was to awake and couldn't sleep
I walked back and forth, and when I got bored with that I just read from II Kings until Shabbat ended. Very fun, as I am picking up where I
left off in reading the whole Tanakh. Over sunday I also beaded for hours, and after staying up a little longer than what is responsible, I finished my project (it's a feather). It has four types of beads,
the main type used being a solid pink bead, followed by a much smaller slightly translucent pink and a translucent silver bead, plus twelve natural pink fresh-water pearls.<br>
Anyway, here is the beading project I finished, followed by some memes I liked and the week's Tefillin photos (and yes, I do really wear white every day)<br>
