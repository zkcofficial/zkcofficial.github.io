# Ethnographic Odessey: Genesis 1
<hr>

### This game is called EO: Genesis 1 because this is the first book in the story of Ethnographic Odessey, a game which follows the affairs of the world, who rules over it, and what the cultures and languages of these people adhere to.


<hr>


Part 1: The world is formed<br>
This is the earth, Called in the language 'Ay'<br>

Water is added, and with the arrival of water to earth comes life. As tribes emerge, they elect their rulers, the kings of old.
<br>
The first kingdom, formed of the 3 tribes who setted along the mountains where the plains and rivers meet, was the dominion of 
the king 'We'. <br>
The next kingdom was formed by 3 tribes who had settled further northwest of We, along the rivers. This kingdom was the dominion of the king
'Seae'. <br>
The thrid kingdom was formed by the 4 tribes on the northern side of the great Southern river fork. This is the dominion of 
king 'Um'.
![attachment (31)](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c2ba774c-1a9e-45bd-8eeb-b7007cdacb65)

In the year 60, the king We of the kingdom We saw the wealth of the kingdom Seae brought by the abundant salmon from the river
'Wuh'. So the king of We gathered his forces and marched on Seae. The battle was fought before the city of Seae, in the plains 
ouside the city, during which the king was killed, and during the battle, the king's heir Seae'e was killed by the king of We's second son,
Im'ea. While the fighting took place, the king of Seae's second son, Eiseae rallied the forces who had stayed behind in the city,
and they plus some of the cities women and children who could flee marched across the river Wuh, situated slightly north along the
river 'Wuu'. There they founded a city called 'Eiseae', after the leader who led them there. Meanwhile, back in the city Seae proper,
king We captured the city with his sons, and he gave as an inheritance to his son Im'ea the entire city of Seae for his bravery.
<br>
By the year 70, the kings of old had died. In their stead, the princes of the world rose to power and accepted their positions.
These princes were: We'e, firstborn of king We, and U'we, thirdborn of king We. They inhereted the city of 'We', becoming duel monarchs. 
The second born of king We, prince Im'ea, recieved the city of 'Seae' as his inheritance for his bravery in battle. The prince
Seae'e, firstborn of king Seae, was killed in battle along with his father. Prince Eiseae, second born of king Seae, founded the city 
'Eiseae', which he took took as his portion. And prince Um'e, only child of king Um, inherited the city of 'Um' as his inheritance.
