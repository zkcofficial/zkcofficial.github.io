### Dutchess Ibm'Umhe

![attachment (45)](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b527370c-de1c-40d9-adf1-aae872fb5422)
(Ii'pomwa Ibm'Umhe'Ium is her title, meaning Dutchess Ibm'Umhe of Ium in the Um tradition)
<hr>

<br>

Lore: Princess Ibm'Umhe was born illegitimate to her father king Um'e. Not wanting to see his daughter suffer unnessicary hardship, king Um'e created the small State of "Ium" for her to control, letting her still have the title of 'Dutchess' in the process. Her father then went on to give her hand in marrige to prince Ee'Eiseae of the kingdom Eiseae, letting him transform into a Duke as well. This union was a perect match, as they loved each other very much. With their union, they bonded the ancestral houses of Um and Seae, helping the two countries of Um and Eiseae establish deeper relations.
