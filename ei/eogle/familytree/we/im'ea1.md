# FamilyTree

### King Im'ea

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/88acaa5e-6ba8-4906-8454-772042456201)
(Em'w'mwa Im'ea'Seae is his title, meaning King Im'ea of Seae in the We tradition)
<hr>

Lore: King Im'ea is seen here with a raised crown in the style of the previous ruling dynasty (Seae)'s crown, so as to be better accepted by the local populas and be seen as a more legitamite ruler. He and his father, king We of of the city kingdom 'We' conqured this city 'Seae', killing it's king and capturing the city. The former prince Im'ea was gifted this city as his personel domain, becaoming king after his father's death. His crown has four diagonal lines across the main body of the crown, as a reminder of his father We's four pronged crown. He also adopted his neice Princess, Iu'Wehe daughter of king We'e of We, gifting her the domain of Eseae. He died in the year 199 of old age.
