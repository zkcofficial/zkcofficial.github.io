### King Im'ea

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/88acaa5e-6ba8-4906-8454-772042456201)

<hr>

Lore: King Im'ea is seen here with a raised crown in the style of the previous ruling dynasty (Seae)'s crown, so as to be better accepted by the local populas and seen as a more legitamite ruler. He and his father, king We of of the city kingdom 'We' conqured this city 'Seae', killing it's king and capturing the city. The former prince Im'ea was gifted this city as his personel domain, becaoming king after his father's death. His crown has four diagonal lines across the main body of the hat, as a reminder of his father We's four pronged crown.<br>
