Lore: Prince Ee'Eiseae was in competition for his father's throne with his two brothers, but seeing the situation he decided to take up the proposition given by king Um'e to marry his daughter Dutchess Ibm'Umhe and gain combined control of her domain, giving up his claim to the thrown of his father Eiseae in the process and settling in the country of Um'e, becoming Duke Ee'Eiseae of Ium.
