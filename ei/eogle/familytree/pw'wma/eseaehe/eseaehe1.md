### King Eseaehe

<i>Image</I> 
<br>
(Em'w'omwa Eseaehe'Eae is his title, meaning King Eseaehe of Eae in the Seae tradition. The overwhelming tradition among the Pw'wma is to depict thier familial crest and title as opposed to portrait)<br>
His name Eseaehe is a reference to the historic prince Seae'e, who was a member of the founding Seae dynasty of the kingdom of Seae. His name translates to 'Large/manly Seae'e'; He was named this by his parents so he would be valiant and large like the older prince Seae'e.

<hr>

<br>

Lore: He was the first king of the kingdom of Eae after fleeing from the home kingdom of Seae with some of his followers. His family were traditional loyalists in his homeland of the kingdom of Seae to the founding dynasty, 'Seae'. Once the war started, which was started by members of the 'We' dynasty against thier cousins to sieze power, Eseaehe took the opprotunity to leave the kingdom along with some other leaders and his followers and settle north-east of the Ea mountains, creating the kingdom of Eae.
