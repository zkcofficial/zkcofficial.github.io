### King Eseaehe

<i>Image</I> 
<br>
(Em'w'omwa Eseaehe'Eae is his title, meaning King Eseaehe of Eae in the Seae tradition. The overwhelming tradition among the Pw'wma is to depict thier familial crest and title as opposed to portrait)<br>
His name Eseaehe is a reference to the historic prince Seae'e, who was a member of the founding Seae dynasty of the kingdom of Seae.

<hr>

<br>

Lore: He was the first king of the kingdom of Eae after fleeing from the home kingdom of Seae with some of his followers.
