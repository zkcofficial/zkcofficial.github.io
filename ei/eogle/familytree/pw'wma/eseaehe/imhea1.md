### King Imhea

<i>Image not available</I> 
<br>
(Em'w'omwa Imhea'Eae is his title, meaning King Imhea of Eae in the Seae tradition. The overwhelming tradition among the Pw'wma is to depict thier familial crest and title as opposed to portrait)<br>
He is named Imhea by his father king Eseaehe of Eae in honor of his wartime ally and friend the now king Imhea of the kingdom Oae. The name has its roots in the historic figure king Imhea of the kingdom of Seae (of the We dynasty).

<hr>

<br>

Lore: He was the second king of the kingdom of Eae, inheriting the kingdom after his father Eseaehe's passing.
