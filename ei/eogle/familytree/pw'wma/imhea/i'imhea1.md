### King I'Imhea

<i>Image not available</I> 
<br>
(Em'w'omwa I'Imhea'Oae is his title, meaning King I'Imhea of Oae in the Seae tradition. The overwhelming tradition among the Pw'wma is to depict thier familial crest and title as opposed to portrait)<br>
His name I'Imhea means "Small Imhea" or "Not as great/in honor of Imhea".

<hr>

<br>

Lore: He was the second king of the kingdom of Oae, and is son of Imhea, former king of the kingdom of Oae.
