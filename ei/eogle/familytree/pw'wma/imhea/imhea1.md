### King Imhea

<i>Image</I> 
<br>
(Em'w'omwa Imhea'Oae is his title, meaning King Imhea of Oae in the Seae tradition. The overwhelming tradition among the Pw'wma is to depict thier familial crest and title as opposed to portrait)<br>
His name Imhea is based off the historic king Imhea from the kingdom Seae.

<hr>

<br>

Lore: He was the first king of the kingdom of Oae after fleeing from the home kingdom of Seae with some of his followers.
