### King Eeuo

<i>Image</I> 
<br>
(Em'w'omwa Eeuo'Ea is his title, meaning King Eeuo of Ea in the Seae tradition. The overwhelming tradition among the Pw'wma is to depict thier familial crest and title as opposed to portrait)<br>
His name Eeuo means 'Large male who causes immediate benefit'.

<hr>

<br>

Lore: He was the first king of the kingdom of Ea after fleeing from the home kingdom of Seae with some of his followers.
