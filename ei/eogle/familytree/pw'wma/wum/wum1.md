### King Wum

<i>Image</I> 
<br>
(Em'w'omwa Wum'Iae is his title, meaning King Wum of Iae in the Seae tradition)<br>
His name Wum means "Very beneficial human" because his parents wanted him to be helpful to society.

<hr>

<br>

Lore: He was the first king of the kingdom of Iam after fleeing from the home kingdom of Seae with some of his followers.
